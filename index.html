A Pleasure to Make Your Acquaintance:
my name is kat sauma.

Hometown, Homebase, Homefree

Born and raised in New Jersey, in what I call the backyard of New York City, I am what is referred to as a "bridge and tunnel kid". Does this bring me any sense of purpose? Not really, but it does give me a sense of my identity. On the weekends I would take the train and sleep on my cousin's couch, getting into trouble and hanging out on rooftops. Now I don't have the same feeling of excitement for NYC I used to have and moved to Los Angeles three years ago.

    Places I have lived:
  1. Warren, NJ
  2. Columbus, Ohio
  3. Berlin, Germany
  4. New York City, NY
  5. Los Angeles, CA

Making Mediums

One thing remained the same no matter where I have lived, I have always found community through my work as a dancer and choreographer. It doesn't always pay the bills, but it has brought me friendship, connection to many cities worldwide, giving me purpose, identity, and creative freedom. Now I am exploring a new form of work as a software engineer and look forward to meshing my ongoing passion for dance with a newfound love in coding.

Dance & Technology

So far some of the work I have devised combines dance and tech. The pieces have been performed in NYC, Brooklyn, Columbus, Ohio, Los Angeles, Berlin, Germany, and Tallinn, Estonia.
